VIVEKANANDHA TRUST was established in 2014 by two young professionals, D. Balu and his friend, who were convinced that even the stubborn, endemic poverty of rural India can be solved. They believed that well-educated professionals working within communities can bring both the empathy and knowledge needed to help poor people improve their lives.

Our vision and mission is to achieve the holistic empowerment of women, people with disabilities, and children. It strives for a society where their rights are upheld and given equal importance. The trust works at the grassroots level to uplift the most deprived sections of society, including women, children, Dalits, small and marginal farmers, tribals, and landless agricultural laborers.
